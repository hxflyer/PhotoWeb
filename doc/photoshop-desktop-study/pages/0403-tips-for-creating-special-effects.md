# Tips for Creating Special Effects

- Entry Type: Creative filter reference
- Category: Effects and filters > Get started with filters
- Source: https://helpx.adobe.com/photoshop/desktop/effects-filters/get-started-filters/tips-for-creating-special-effects.html

**Function Summary**
Special effects combine filters, masks, blend modes, selections, layer styles, and Smart Objects to create controlled visual treatments.

**What This Function Describes**
- Work non-destructively with Smart Objects and duplicate layers.
- Limit effects with selections, masks, and Smart Filter masks.
- Combine multiple filters with blend modes and opacity.
- Build effects in stages rather than relying on one extreme setting.

**Where You Find It**
- `Filter` menu, `Layers` panel, masks, blend modes, and adjustment layers.

**Expected Result**
- Users can design repeatable effects that stay editable and visually controlled.
