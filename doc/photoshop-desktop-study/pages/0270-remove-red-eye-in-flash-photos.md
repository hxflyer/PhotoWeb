# Remove Red Eye in Flash Photos

- Entry Type: Red Eye correction workflow
- Category: Repair and retouch > Clean and restore images
- Source: https://helpx.adobe.com/photoshop/desktop/repair-retouch/clean-restore-images/remove-red-eye-in-flash-photos.html

**Function Summary**
The `Red Eye Tool` darkens and corrects flash reflections in pupils.

**Where You Find It**
- `Red Eye Tool` in the retouch tool group

**Primary Interface Elements**
- `Pupil Size`
- `Darken Amount`

**How To Use It**
1. Select the `Red Eye Tool`.
2. Set `Pupil Size` and `Darken Amount`.
3. Click the red or white reflection in the pupil.

**Expected Result**
- The flash reflection is replaced with a more natural pupil appearance.
