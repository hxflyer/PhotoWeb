# Export Video Files or Image Sequences

- Entry Type: Video render workflow
- Category: Save and export
- Source: https://helpx.adobe.com/photoshop/desktop/save-export/export-video-files-image-sequences.html

**Function Summary**
Render Video exports timeline animation or video content as a video file or numbered image sequence.

**Where You Find It**
- `File > Export > Render Video`.

**Primary Interface Elements**
- Render Video dialog.
- Destination folder.
- Format and preset menus.
- Size, frame rate, work area, and range controls.
- Image sequence options.

**How To Use It**
1. Open the document with timeline animation or video content.
2. Choose `File > Export > Render Video`.
3. Select destination, format, preset, size, and frame rate.
4. Choose full timeline or work area range.
5. Render the output.

**Expected Result**
- Photoshop exports a video file or image sequence from the timeline.
