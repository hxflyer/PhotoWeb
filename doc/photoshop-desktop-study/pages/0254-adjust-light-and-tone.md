# Adjust Light and Tone

- Entry Type: Topic hub
- Category: Repair and retouch
- Source: https://helpx.adobe.com/photoshop/desktop/repair-retouch/adjust-light-tone.html

**Function Summary**
This hub groups features for local and layer-based control of brightness, contrast, exposure, and tonal emphasis.

**What This Entry Is For**
- connects blend modes, Dodge, Burn, and vignette-style workflows
- helps users choose between painting-based tonal edits and adjustment-style edits
- supports targeted light shaping during retouching

**Primary Function Areas**
- blending modes
- Dodge and Burn tools
- vignette quick actions

**Expected Result**
- Users can locate tone-control workflows used to brighten, darken, blend, or focus image areas.
