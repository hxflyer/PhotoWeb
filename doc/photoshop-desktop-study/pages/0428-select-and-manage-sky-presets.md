# Select and Manage Sky Presets

- Entry Type: Sky preset management workflow
- Category: Effects and filters
- Source: https://helpx.adobe.com/photoshop/desktop/effects-filters/select-manage-sky-presets.html

**Function Summary**
Sky preset management organizes, imports, exports, deletes, and selects sky images used by the Sky Replacement command.

**Where You Find It**
- `Edit > Sky Replacement`, sky picker menu.

**Primary Interface Elements**
- Sky preset thumbnail grid.
- Preset groups.
- Add/import sky controls.
- Rename, delete, export, and group management options.

**How To Use It**
1. Open `Edit > Sky Replacement`.
2. Open the sky picker.
3. Select an existing sky or import a new sky image.
4. Organize skies into groups if needed.
5. Use preset menu commands to rename, delete, or export sky collections.

**Expected Result**
- The Sky Replacement dialog uses the selected or newly imported sky preset.
