# Inverse Selection

- Entry Type: Selection inversion command
- Category: Make selections > Refine and modify selections
- Source: https://helpx.adobe.com/photoshop/desktop/make-selections/refine-modify-selections/inverse-selection.html

**Function Summary**
`Inverse` swaps selected and unselected image areas.

**What This Function Does**
- turns a background selection into a subject selection
- flips the current targeting logic
- saves time when the easier area to select is the opposite of the desired edit region

**Where You Find It**
- `Select > Inverse`

**How To Use It**
1. Make an initial selection.
2. Choose `Select > Inverse`.

**Mouse, Keyboard, and Menu Input**
- Menu path: `Select > Inverse`

**User Interface Behavior**
- Photoshop immediately replaces the current selection with its opposite area.

**Expected Result**
- Everything previously unselected becomes selected, and everything previously selected becomes unselected.
