# Darken the Edges of Your Image to Bring Focus to the Center

- Entry Type: Vignette quick action workflow
- Category: Repair and retouch > Adjust light and tone
- Source: https://helpx.adobe.com/photoshop/desktop/repair-retouch/adjust-light-tone/darken-the-edges-of-your-image-to-bring-focus-to-its-center.html

**Function Summary**
The vignette quick action darkens image edges to guide attention toward the center.

**Where You Find It**
- `Discover > Quick Actions > Add a vignette`

**Primary Interface Elements**
- `Search`
- `Discover`
- `Quick Actions`
- `Add a vignette`
- `Apply`
- `Refresh`
- `Revert`
- `Camera Raw Filter`

**How To Use It**
1. Open the `Discover` panel from the search icon.
2. Browse `Quick Actions`.
3. Choose `Add a vignette`.
4. Click `Apply`.
5. Refine through `Filter > Camera Raw Filter` if needed.

**Expected Result**
- The image edges darken, creating stronger visual focus around the center.
