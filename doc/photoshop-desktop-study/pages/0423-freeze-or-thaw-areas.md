# Freeze or Thaw Areas

- Entry Type: Liquify mask workflow
- Category: Effects and filters > Distortion
- Source: https://helpx.adobe.com/photoshop/desktop/effects-filters/distortion/freeze-thaw-areas.html

**Function Summary**
Freeze and Thaw tools protect or release areas inside Liquify so distortion affects only intended regions.

**Where You Find It**
- `Filter > Liquify`, `Freeze Mask Tool` and `Thaw Mask Tool`.

**Primary Interface Elements**
- Freeze mask overlay.
- Freeze Mask and Thaw Mask brush tools.
- Mask options such as invert, clear, or load where available.

**How To Use It**
1. Open Liquify.
2. Paint with the `Freeze Mask Tool` over areas to protect.
3. Distort nearby areas with Liquify tools.
4. Paint with the `Thaw Mask Tool` to remove protection where needed.
5. Confirm the Liquify result.

**Expected Result**
- Frozen areas resist distortion while thawed areas can be reshaped.
