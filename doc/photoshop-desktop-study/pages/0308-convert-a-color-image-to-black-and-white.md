# Convert a Color Image to Black and White

- Entry Type: Black & White adjustment workflow
- Category: Adjust color > Color effects and techniques
- Source: https://helpx.adobe.com/photoshop/desktop/adjust-color/color-effects-techniques/convert-a-color-image-to-black-and-white.html

**Function Summary**
The `Black & White` adjustment converts color images to grayscale while preserving control over how source colors map to gray tones.

**Where You Find It**
- `Layer > New Adjustment Layer > Black & White`

**Primary Interface Elements**
- `Preset`
- `Auto`
- `Tint`
- color sliders
- on-image adjustment control
- preset save menu

**How To Use It**
1. Create a `Black & White` adjustment layer.
2. Choose a preset or use `Auto`.
3. Adjust color sliders to control tonal mapping.
4. Enable `Tint` if a toned black-and-white effect is wanted.

**Expected Result**
- The image becomes black and white with adjustable tonal response by original color.
