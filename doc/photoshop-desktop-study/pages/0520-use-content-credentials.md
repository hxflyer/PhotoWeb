# Use Content Credentials

- Entry Type: Content Credentials workflow
- Category: Save and export > Metadata, CSS, and Content Credentials
- Source: https://helpx.adobe.com/photoshop/desktop/save-export/metadata-css-content-credentials/use-content-credentials.html

**Function Summary**
Content Credentials attach provenance information to exported content, such as creator identity choices, editing history, assets used, and AI usage where supported.

**Where You Find It**
- Content Credentials controls in Photoshop, export dialogs, or related panel/preferences where available.

**Primary Interface Elements**
- Content Credentials toggle.
- Connected account or identity options.
- Included information controls.
- Preview and export status.

**How To Use It**
1. Open Content Credentials controls.
2. Enable Content Credentials for the document or export.
3. Choose which information to include.
4. Preview the credentials.
5. Export with credentials attached or published according to available options.

**Expected Result**
- The exported content includes selected provenance metadata.
