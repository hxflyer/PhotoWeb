# Fringe Pixels Around a Selection

- Entry Type: Matting reference
- Category: Make selections > Refine and modify selections
- Source: https://helpx.adobe.com/photoshop/desktop/make-selections/refine-modify-selections/fringe-pixels-around-a-selection.html

**Function Summary**
This reference explains why halo or fringe pixels appear around pasted and anti-aliased selections and which commands remove them.

**What This Entry Is For**
- explains selection halos after cutout or paste operations
- distinguishes between anti-aliased edge quality and unwanted background contamination
- points to the proper matting commands for cleanup

**Primary Cleanup Methods**
- `Color Decontaminate`
- `Defringe`
- `Remove Black Matte`
- `Remove White Matte`
- advanced blending sliders

**Expected Result**
- Users can diagnose whether an edge problem is caused by fringe color contamination and choose the right correction method.
