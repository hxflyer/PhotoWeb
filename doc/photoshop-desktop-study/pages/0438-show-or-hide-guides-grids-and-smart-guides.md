# Show or Hide Guides, Grids, and Smart Guides

- Entry Type: Overlay visibility workflow
- Category: Grids, guides, and measurement
- Source: https://helpx.adobe.com/photoshop/desktop/grids-guides-measurement/show-hide-guides-grids-smart-guides.html

**Function Summary**
Overlay visibility controls display or hide guides, grids, and Smart Guides without deleting them.

**Where You Find It**
- `View > Show > Guides`.
- `View > Show > Grid`.
- `View > Show > Smart Guides`.
- `View > Extras`, shortcut `Cmd+H` on macOS or `Ctrl+H` on Windows.

**Primary Interface Elements**
- Visible guide lines.
- Grid overlay.
- Smart Guide alignment hints.

**How To Use It**
1. Open the `View > Show` submenu.
2. Toggle the overlay type you want to show or hide.
3. Use `View > Extras` to hide or show multiple non-printing extras at once.

**Expected Result**
- The chosen overlays appear or disappear while the document content remains unchanged.
