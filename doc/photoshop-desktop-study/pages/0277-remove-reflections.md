# Remove Reflections

- Entry Type: Reflection Removal workflow
- Category: Repair and retouch > Clean and restore images
- Source: https://helpx.adobe.com/photoshop/desktop/repair-retouch/clean-restore-images/remove-reflections.html

**Function Summary**
`Reflection Removal` reduces large glass or window reflections from a photo.

**Where You Find It**
- `Edit > Reflection Removal`

**Primary Interface Elements**
- `Create separate reflection layer`
- `Quality`
- `Preview`
- `Standard`
- `Best`
- `OK`

**How To Use It**
1. Open the image.
2. Select the layer containing the reflection.
3. Choose `Edit > Reflection Removal`.
4. Choose output and quality settings.
5. Click `OK`.

**User Interface Behavior**
- Keeping `Create separate reflection layer` enabled preserves the original and creates separate cleaned/reflection layers.

**Expected Result**
- Large full-image reflections are reduced while preserving an editable layer structure.
