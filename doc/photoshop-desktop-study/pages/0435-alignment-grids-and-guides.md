# Alignment Grids and Guides

- Entry Type: Alignment reference
- Category: Grids, guides, and measurement
- Source: https://helpx.adobe.com/photoshop/desktop/grids-guides-measurement/alignment-grids-guides.html

**Function Summary**
Alignment grids and guides provide non-printing visual references for placing layers, selections, shapes, and type accurately.

**Where You Find It**
- `View > Show > Grid`.
- `View > Guides`.
- `View > Snap` and `View > Snap To`.
- Rulers with `Cmd+R` on macOS or `Ctrl+R` on Windows.

**Primary Interface Elements**
- Grid overlay.
- Horizontal and vertical guides.
- Smart Guide magenta alignment hints.
- Snap behavior.

**Expected Result**
- Artwork can be aligned visually and mechanically without adding printed content to the image.
