# Remove Guides

- Entry Type: Guide removal workflow
- Category: Grids, guides, and measurement
- Source: https://helpx.adobe.com/photoshop/desktop/grids-guides-measurement/remove-guides.html

**Function Summary**
Guide removal deletes one guide or clears all guides from the document.

**Where You Find It**
- Drag a guide back onto the ruler.
- `View > Guides > Clear Guides`.

**Primary Interface Elements**
- Existing guide lines.
- Rulers.
- Clear Guides command.

**How To Use It**
1. Show guides.
2. To remove one guide, unlock guides and drag it off the canvas into the ruler.
3. To remove all guides, choose `View > Guides > Clear Guides`.

**Expected Result**
- Selected guides disappear and no longer affect snapping.
