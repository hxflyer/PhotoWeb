# Display the Brush Settings Panel and Brush Options

- Entry Type: Brush settings interface reference
- Category: Apply painting techniques > Brushes and presets
- Source: https://helpx.adobe.com/photoshop/desktop/apply-painting-techniques/brushes-presets/display-brush-settings-panel-and-brush-options.html

**Function Summary**
The `Brush Settings` panel exposes detailed controls for brush tip shape, dynamics, scattering, texture, smoothing, and related behaviors.

**Where You Find It**
- `Window > Brush Settings`
- `Window > Brushes`

**Expected Result**
- Users can inspect and modify brush behavior beyond the simple size and hardness controls.
