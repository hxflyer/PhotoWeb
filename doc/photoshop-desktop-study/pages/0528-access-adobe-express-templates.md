# Access Adobe Express Templates

- Entry Type: Adobe Express integration workflow
- Category: Share and collaborate > App integrations
- Source: https://helpx.adobe.com/photoshop/desktop/share-collaborate/app-integrations/access-adobe-express-templates.html

**Function Summary**
Adobe Express template access lets users start from template-based layouts and assets connected to Express workflows.

**Where You Find It**
- Photoshop Home screen or integration entry points where Express templates are offered.

**Primary Interface Elements**
- Template browser.
- Category/search controls.
- Template preview.
- Open or customize command.

**How To Use It**
1. Open the Express template entry point.
2. Browse or search for a template.
3. Preview the design.
4. Open or customize it through the available integration workflow.

**Expected Result**
- A template-based design becomes available for further editing or handoff.
