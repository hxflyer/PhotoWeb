# Neural Filters

- Entry Type: Topic hub
- Category: Effects and filters
- Source: https://helpx.adobe.com/photoshop/desktop/effects-filters/neural-filters.html

**Function Summary**
Neural Filters use machine-learning models to apply portrait, color, restoration, style, and enhancement effects through a dedicated panel.

**Where You Find It**
- `Filter > Neural Filters`.

**Primary Interface Elements**
- Neural Filters panel.
- Filter list and download/cloud indicators.
- Filter-specific sliders.
- Output menu for current layer, duplicate layer, Smart Filter, or new layer workflows where available.

**Expected Result**
- Users can apply AI-powered image transformations while choosing an output method that fits the edit.
