# Use the Measurement Log for Performing Measurements

- Entry Type: Measurement log workflow
- Category: Grids, guides, and measurement
- Source: https://helpx.adobe.com/photoshop/desktop/grids-guides-measurement/use-measurement-log-performing-measurements.html

**Function Summary**
The Measurement Log records measured values from selections, rulers, counts, and analysis data points.

**Where You Find It**
- `Window > Measurement Log`.
- `Image > Analysis > Record Measurements`.

**Primary Interface Elements**
- Measurement Log panel.
- Record Measurements command.
- Data point columns.
- Export or clear log controls.

**How To Use It**
1. Open `Window > Measurement Log`.
2. Choose the measurement data points to record.
3. Make a selection, count, or ruler measurement.
4. Choose `Record Measurements`.
5. Export or clear the log as needed.

**Expected Result**
- Photoshop adds measurement rows with the selected data point values.
