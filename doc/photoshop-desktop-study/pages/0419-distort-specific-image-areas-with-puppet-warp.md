# Distort Specific Image Areas with Puppet Warp

- Entry Type: Puppet Warp workflow
- Category: Effects and filters > Distortion
- Source: https://helpx.adobe.com/photoshop/desktop/effects-filters/distortion/distort-specific-image-areas-puppet-warp.html

**Function Summary**
Puppet Warp places pins on a mesh so specific image areas can be bent, posed, or repositioned while other areas stay anchored.

**Where You Find It**
- `Edit > Puppet Warp`.

**Primary Interface Elements**
- Mesh overlay.
- Pins.
- Mode, Density, Expansion, and Show Mesh options.
- Pin rotation and delete controls.

**How To Use It**
1. Select the layer or Smart Object.
2. Choose `Edit > Puppet Warp`.
3. Add pins to anchor stable areas and movable joints.
4. Drag pins to reshape the subject.
5. Commit the warp.

**Expected Result**
- Selected parts of the image bend around the placed pins.
