# Manage Measurement Logs

- Entry Type: Measurement log management workflow
- Category: Grids, guides, and measurement
- Source: https://helpx.adobe.com/photoshop/desktop/grids-guides-measurement/manage-measurement-logs.html

**Function Summary**
Measurement log management clears, exports, reviews, and organizes recorded measurement rows.

**Where You Find It**
- `Window > Measurement Log`.
- Measurement Log panel menu.

**Primary Interface Elements**
- Log rows and columns.
- Export, clear, select, and delete controls.
- Data point columns.

**How To Use It**
1. Open the Measurement Log.
2. Review recorded measurements.
3. Delete unneeded rows or clear the log.
4. Export measurement data for reporting or analysis.

**Expected Result**
- Measurement records are organized, saved externally, or cleared from the panel.
