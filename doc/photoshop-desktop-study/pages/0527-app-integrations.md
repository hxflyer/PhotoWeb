# App Integrations

- Entry Type: Topic hub
- Category: Share and collaborate
- Source: https://helpx.adobe.com/photoshop/desktop/share-collaborate/app-integrations.html

**Function Summary**
App integrations connect Photoshop with Adobe Express, Firefly, video generation workflows, Illustrator, and other Adobe creative tools.

**Where You Find It**
- Home screen, contextual task controls, share/export commands, and app-specific integration commands.

**Primary Interface Elements**
- Template browser, Firefly refinement controls, image-to-video workflow, and open-in-app commands.

**Expected Result**
- Users can continue Photoshop work in related Adobe applications or bring generated assets back into Photoshop workflows.
