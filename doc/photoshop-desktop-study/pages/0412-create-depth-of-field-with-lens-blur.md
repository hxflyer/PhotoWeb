# Create Depth of Field with Lens Blur

- Entry Type: Lens Blur workflow
- Category: Effects and filters > Blur and sharpen
- Source: https://helpx.adobe.com/photoshop/desktop/effects-filters/blur-sharpen/create-depth-field-lens-blur.html

**Function Summary**
Lens Blur simulates camera depth of field by blurring areas according to a selection, mask, depth map, or focus settings.

**Where You Find It**
- `Filter > Blur > Lens Blur` or Lens Blur options in supported blur workflows.

**Primary Interface Elements**
- Preview pane.
- Blur radius and iris controls.
- Depth map source settings.
- Specular highlight and noise controls where available.

**How To Use It**
1. Prepare a selection or depth mask if you need controlled focus.
2. Select the target layer or Smart Object.
3. Open Lens Blur.
4. Set the focus/depth source and blur amount.
5. Adjust highlights and noise so the blur matches the image.

**Expected Result**
- The image appears to have a shallower depth of field with selected areas in focus.
