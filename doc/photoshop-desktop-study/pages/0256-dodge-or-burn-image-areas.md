# Dodge or Burn Image Areas

- Entry Type: Local exposure painting workflow
- Category: Repair and retouch > Adjust light and tone
- Source: https://helpx.adobe.com/photoshop/desktop/repair-retouch/adjust-light-tone/dodge-or-burn-image-areas.html

**Function Summary**
The `Dodge` and `Burn` tools selectively lighten or darken brushed areas of an image.

**What This Function Does**
- `Dodge` lightens local areas
- `Burn` darkens local areas
- targets shadows, midtones, or highlights
- applies strength through exposure and brush settings

**Where You Find It**
- `Dodge Tool`
- `Burn Tool`

**Primary Interface Elements**
- brush preset
- `Range`
- `Shadows`
- `Midtones`
- `Highlights`
- `Exposure`
- `Protect Tones`

**How To Use It**
1. Select `Dodge` or `Burn`.
2. Choose brush size and hardness.
3. Select a tonal `Range`.
4. Set `Exposure`.
5. Enable `Protect Tones` for more natural color preservation.
6. Paint over the area to brighten or darken.

**Expected Result**
- Local exposure changes are painted into the image area.
