# Replace the Sky in Images

- Entry Type: Sky Replacement workflow
- Category: Effects and filters
- Source: https://helpx.adobe.com/photoshop/desktop/effects-filters/replace-sky-images.html

**Function Summary**
Sky Replacement detects the sky in a photo and replaces it with a chosen sky while generating masks and color adjustments.

**Where You Find It**
- `Edit > Sky Replacement`.

**Primary Interface Elements**
- Sky picker and sky preset groups.
- Shift Edge, Fade Edge, Brightness, Temperature, Scale, Flip, and lighting controls.
- Output options for new layers or duplicate layer workflows.

**How To Use It**
1. Open a landscape or outdoor image.
2. Choose `Edit > Sky Replacement`.
3. Pick a sky preset or import a sky image.
4. Refine edge blend, scale, and lighting.
5. Choose an output option and click `OK`.

**Expected Result**
- Photoshop replaces the sky and creates editable layers or masks for the composite.
