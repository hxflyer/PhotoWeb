# Blur and Sharpen Filters

- Entry Type: Blur and sharpen reference
- Category: Effects and filters
- Source: https://helpx.adobe.com/photoshop/desktop/effects-filters/blur-sharpen-filters.html

**Function Summary**
Blur and sharpen filters control perceived focus, edge contrast, depth, softness, motion, and detail emphasis.

**Where You Find It**
- `Filter > Blur`, `Filter > Blur Gallery`, and `Filter > Sharpen`.
- Blur Tool and Sharpen Tool in the toolbar.

**Primary Interface Elements**
- Blur radius and gallery pins.
- Sharpen Amount/Radius/Threshold controls.
- Brush controls for local blur or sharpen tools.

**Expected Result**
- Users can soften areas, simulate depth of field, reduce detail, or increase edge clarity.
