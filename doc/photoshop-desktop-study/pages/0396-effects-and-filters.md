# Effects and Filters

- Entry Type: Topic hub
- Category: Photoshop Desktop Help
- Source: https://helpx.adobe.com/photoshop/desktop/effects-filters.html

**Function Summary**
Effects and filters modify image appearance with sharpening, blur, distortion, stylization, AI enhancement, and non-destructive Smart Filter workflows.

**Where You Find It**
- `Filter` menu, `Layer > Layer Style`, toolbar retouching tools, and contextual task options.

**Primary Interface Elements**
- Filter dialogs and preview windows.
- Smart Object and Smart Filter entries in the `Layers` panel.
- Filter Gallery, Liquify workspace, Neural Filters panel, Sky Replacement dialog, and tool options bars.

**Expected Result**
- Users can choose the correct effect workflow for image correction, creative styling, distortion, or AI-assisted enhancement.
