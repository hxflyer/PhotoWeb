**Function Summary**
This hub groups Photoshop features for trimming composition, extending canvas with the crop workflow, straightening tilted images, and correcting perspective during crop operations.

**What This Entry Is For**
- directs users to crop-related tools and settings
- separates standard crop, straightening, perspective crop, and content-aware crop behavior
- acts as the entry point for framing workflows

**Primary Function Areas**
- basic crop
- crop tool options
- canvas expansion with crop handles
- Content-Aware Fill during crop
- straightening
- perspective correction

**Where You Access These Tools**
- `Crop Tool` in the toolbar
- crop-related options in the options bar
- `Perspective Crop Tool` nested under the crop tool group

**Expected Result**
- Users can navigate to the correct crop-related feature based on whether they need trimming, rotation correction, or perspective correction.
