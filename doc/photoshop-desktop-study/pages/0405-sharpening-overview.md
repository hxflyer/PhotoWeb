# Sharpening Overview

- Entry Type: Sharpening reference
- Category: Effects and filters
- Source: https://helpx.adobe.com/photoshop/desktop/effects-filters/sharpening-overview.html

**Function Summary**
Sharpening increases edge contrast to make details appear clearer without actually adding new image detail.

**Where You Find It**
- `Filter > Sharpen`.
- `Filter > Sharpen > Smart Sharpen`.
- `Filter > Sharpen > Unsharp Mask`.
- `Sharpen Tool` in the toolbar.

**Primary Interface Elements**
- Amount, Radius, Reduce Noise, and shadow/highlight controls in Smart Sharpen.
- Amount, Radius, and Threshold controls in Unsharp Mask.
- Brush settings for the Sharpen Tool.

**Expected Result**
- Edges appear crisper, with the risk of halos or noise if sharpening is too strong.
