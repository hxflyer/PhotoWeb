# Artistic and Stylize Filters

- Entry Type: Creative filter reference
- Category: Effects and filters
- Source: https://helpx.adobe.com/photoshop/desktop/effects-filters/artistic-stylize-filters.html

**Function Summary**
Artistic and Stylize filters transform images into painterly, graphic, edge-based, texture, or illustration-like effects.

**Where You Find It**
- `Filter > Filter Gallery`.
- `Filter > Stylize`.
- Related creative filter submenus.

**Primary Interface Elements**
- Filter Gallery preview and categories.
- Stylize filter dialogs.
- Effect settings sliders and previews.

**Expected Result**
- Images receive visible creative treatments such as outlines, textures, painterly marks, glowing edges, or graphic distortion.
