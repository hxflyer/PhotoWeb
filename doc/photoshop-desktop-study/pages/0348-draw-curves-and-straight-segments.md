# Draw Curves and Straight Segments

- Entry Type: Curvature Pen workflow
- Category: Draw shapes and paths > Draw lines and curves
- Source: https://helpx.adobe.com/photoshop/desktop/draw-shapes-paths/draw-lines-curves/draw-curves-and-straight-segments-intuitively.html

**Function Summary**
The `Curvature Pen Tool` creates smooth curves and straight segments by placing points directly on the canvas.

**How To Use It**
1. Select the `Curvature Pen Tool`.
2. Click to place points and create curved segments.
3. Double-click to create or convert corner points for straight segments.
4. Click the first point to close the path.

**Expected Result**
- Photoshop creates an editable path with smooth curves or straight segments.
