# Create a Timeline Animation Workflow

- Entry Type: Timeline animation workflow
- Category: Video and animation
- Source: https://helpx.adobe.com/photoshop/desktop/video-animation/create-timeline-animation-workflow.html

**Function Summary**
Timeline animation builds motion over time with layer tracks, keyframes, video clips, audio, and playback controls.

**Where You Find It**
- `Window > Timeline`, `Create Video Timeline`.

**Primary Interface Elements**
- Time ruler.
- Playhead.
- Layer tracks.
- Keyframe stopwatches and diamonds.
- Playback and render controls.

**How To Use It**
1. Open `Window > Timeline`.
2. Choose `Create Video Timeline`.
3. Arrange layers or video clips in time.
4. Add keyframes for animatable properties.
5. Preview playback and adjust timing.
6. Export or render the animation.

**Expected Result**
- The document plays as a time-based animation or video sequence.
