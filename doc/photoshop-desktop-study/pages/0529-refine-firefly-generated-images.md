# Refine Firefly Generated Images

- Entry Type: Firefly integration workflow
- Category: Share and collaborate > App integrations
- Source: https://helpx.adobe.com/photoshop/desktop/share-collaborate/app-integrations/refine-firefly-generated-images.html

**Function Summary**
Firefly refinement workflows send or continue generated imagery through Photoshop editing tools for compositing, retouching, or further generation.

**Where You Find It**
- Firefly-to-Photoshop integration entry points.
- Generative AI layers and contextual task controls where available.

**Primary Interface Elements**
- Generated image result.
- Open/edit in Photoshop control.
- Layers, masks, generative controls, and adjustment tools.

**How To Use It**
1. Choose a Firefly-generated image or variation.
2. Open it in Photoshop through the integration.
3. Refine with layers, masks, adjustments, retouching, or generative tools.
4. Save or export the finished result.

**Expected Result**
- Firefly output becomes an editable Photoshop composition or asset.
