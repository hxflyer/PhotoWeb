# Overview of Adding Blur to Images

- Entry Type: Blur reference
- Category: Effects and filters > Blur and sharpen
- Source: https://helpx.adobe.com/photoshop/desktop/effects-filters/blur-sharpen/overview-adding-blur-images.html

**Function Summary**
Blur softens detail, reduces focus, simulates depth, creates motion, and directs attention by lowering edge contrast.

**Where You Find It**
- `Filter > Blur`.
- `Filter > Blur Gallery`.
- `Blur Tool` in the toolbar.

**Primary Interface Elements**
- Radius controls.
- Blur Gallery pins and overlays.
- Masked or selected areas.
- Brush size, strength, and hardness for the Blur Tool.

**Expected Result**
- Selected image areas appear softer or less detailed.
