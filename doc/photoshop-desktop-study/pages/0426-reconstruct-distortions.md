# Reconstruct Distortions

- Entry Type: Liquify correction workflow
- Category: Effects and filters > Distortion
- Source: https://helpx.adobe.com/photoshop/desktop/effects-filters/distortion/reconstruct-distortions.html

**Function Summary**
Reconstruct controls reduce, erase, or partially roll back Liquify distortions without closing the Liquify workspace.

**Where You Find It**
- `Filter > Liquify`, `Reconstruct Tool` and reconstruction options.

**Primary Interface Elements**
- Reconstruct Tool brush.
- Reconstruct or Restore All controls.
- Brush pressure and density settings.

**How To Use It**
1. Open Liquify after creating or applying distortion.
2. Select the `Reconstruct Tool`.
3. Paint over distorted areas to move them back toward the original shape.
4. Use stronger reconstruction or restore controls if needed.

**Expected Result**
- Liquify distortion is partially or fully reduced in the target area.
