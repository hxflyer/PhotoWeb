# Remove a Matte from a Selection

- Entry Type: Matte-removal workflow
- Category: Make selections > Refine and modify selections
- Source: https://helpx.adobe.com/photoshop/desktop/make-selections/refine-modify-selections/remove-matte-from-selection.html

**Function Summary**
These matting commands remove black or white edge contamination left around an anti-aliased selection.

**What This Function Does**
- removes black matte contamination
- removes white matte contamination
- improves edge cleanliness after compositing

**Where You Find It**
- `Layer > Matting > Remove Black Matte`
- `Layer > Matting > Remove White Matte`

**How To Use It**
1. Select the affected layer.
2. Choose the matte-removal command that matches the contamination color.

**Expected Result**
- Black or white halo contamination around the selected edge is reduced or removed.
