# Overview of Neural Filters

- Entry Type: Neural Filters reference
- Category: Effects and filters > Neural Filters
- Source: https://helpx.adobe.com/photoshop/desktop/effects-filters/neural-filters/overview-neural-filters.html

**Function Summary**
The Neural Filters workspace lists available AI filters, downloads required models, previews changes, and outputs the result as pixels or editable layers.

**Where You Find It**
- `Filter > Neural Filters`.

**Primary Interface Elements**
- Featured and available filter lists.
- Cloud download icons for filters not yet installed.
- Preview toggle.
- Filter-specific settings.
- Output dropdown.

**Expected Result**
- Users understand the Neural Filters panel structure and how AI filter availability, preview, and output choices work.
